# Initial Requirements: Expense Approval System

*From: CFO & Operations Manager*
*Date: January 10, 2024*
*Format: Email bullet points*

## The List (As Provided)

1. Approvals should be automatic for small amounts
2. Must integrate with QuickBooks
3. Should be fast and easy to use
4. Need approval workflows based on expense type
5. Managers should approve team expenses
6. CFO must approve anything over $5,000
7. Email notifications when approval needed
8. Track who approved what and when
9. Mobile-friendly (people travel)
10. Prevent duplicate submissions
11. Attach receipts (photos from phone)
12. Slack notifications for approvers
13. Expenses should route to right person automatically
14. Need reporting dashboard
15. Must be secure (sensitive financial data)
16. Integrate with our HR system for org chart
17. Handle foreign currency
18. Should work offline

## The Problems

**Conflicts:**
- Item #7 (email) vs. #12 (Slack) - which one?
- Item #1 (automatic) vs. #5 (managers approve) - what's "small"?

**Vague Items:**
- #3 "fast and easy" - how fast? easy for whom?
- #4 "based on expense type" - which types? what rules?
- #13 "right person" - what determines this?

**Missing Info:**
- No success metrics
- No timeline
- No priority (all seem equal)
- No current pain points quantified
