## Using This Template

### 1. Fill out what you know

Don't worry if you can't answer everything. The AI will ask follow-up questions.

### 2. Be specific with numbers

❌ "We have lots of users"
✅ "We have 2,000 active users, 400 are power users"

### 3. Provide evidence

❌ "Users want this"
✅ "30% of support tickets mention this, it's the #2 feature request"

### 4. Start your prompt like this:

```
I want to create a PRD for [project name] using the product-wizard skill.

Here's my business brief:

[paste your filled-out template]

Can you help me turn this into a comprehensive PRD?
```

### 5. Answer follow-up questions

The AI will ask clarifying questions. Answer with specifics, not generalities.

---

## Examples

### Good Specificity

✅ "Reduce approval time from 3 days to 4 hours for 80% of expenses"
✅ "Support 200 concurrent approvers during month-end (peak load)"
✅ "Must integrate with QuickBooks Online via their REST API"

### Bad Vagueness

❌ "Make it faster"
❌ "Handle lots of users"
❌ "Integrate with accounting"
